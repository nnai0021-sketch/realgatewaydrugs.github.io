The Reality

So if its not milk, and not alcohol, what is the real gateway drug? And lets address the elephant in the room – its not cannabis either. The real gateways, the real causes of hard drug addictions are not substances at all. They are conditions.
Poverty. Trauma. Criminalization.
Poverty leaves people without choice, constantly in survival mode. The idea of “escape” from it’s harsh and unforgiving conditions becomes a necessity. In a poor neighbourhood, drugs such as heroin, fentanyl, and cocaine provide an easy, accessible form of relief for the users, and a means to an end for dealers in an environment so utterly devoid of opportunity. Abuse, violence, neglect – trauma, only strengthens one’s desire to self medicate. Then, if you label these people as criminals, it perpetuates what is now an endless cycle. These are the real gateways.
The problem was never cannabis, it was
the system.
